ABOUT

White-Pebble is a custom Minecraft 1.12.2 modded Forge launcher designed to enhance your gaming experience. With a user-friendly interface and seamless integration, White-Pebble brings a new level of convenience to Minecraft modding. The launcher allows you to effortlessly install, manage, and update mods, making it easier than ever to customize your gameplay.

Explore a vast array of mods available through White-Pebble's integrated mod repository, where you can browse and select from a curated collection of popular and community-created modifications. Discover new biomes, mobs, weapons, tools, and much more to enrich your Minecraft world and gameplay.

The launcher ensures compatibility and stability by automatically handling the required dependencies and conflicts between various mods, letting you focus on the fun of playing rather than troubleshooting. White-Pebble also offers an intuitive backup system, enabling you to save your mod configurations and worlds, allowing you to switch between different mod setups with ease.

Stay connected with the White-Pebble community through the launcher's built-in chat and forum features. Share your experiences, seek help, or showcase your modded creations with other players who share the same passion for Minecraft modding.

Experience the limitless possibilities of Minecraft with White-Pebble - a reliable and efficient modded Forge launcher that takes your gaming adventure to a whole new level!
